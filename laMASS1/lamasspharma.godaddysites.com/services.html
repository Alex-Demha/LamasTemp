OUR SERVICES

Sales Representation:

  

Business Operations for private and public markets are headed by one manager, Two Supervisors, and 32 Med reps. & salesmen.

LAMASS PHARMA SDB provides professional sales representation services to pharmaceutical manufacturers seeking to expand their product reach and market share. Our experienced sales team establishes strong relationships with healthcare providers, pharmacies, hospitals, and other key stakeholders to promote and sell products effectively.

For the private market, they follow the promotional activities and all performance of sales, marketing, logistics and distribution related to private market achievements. The promotion is measured on a qualitative and quantitative basis.

For the public market (KIMADIA – MOH.) tender management, they follow the interests of our principals at every stage and communicate the status on every phase, from demand creation till product liquidation.

Marketing and Promotion

  We offer comprehensive marketing and promotional services designed to increase product visibility, awareness, and demand. From developing tailored marketing campaigns to organizing promotional events and educational initiatives, LAMASS PHARMA SDB implements targeted strategies to create buzz around pharmaceutical products and drive sales growth.

Distribution and Logistics

(One manager heads private and public markets & Four employees).

LAMASS PHARMA SDB manages all aspects of product distribution and logistics to ensure timely and efficient delivery to customers. With a robust distribution network and specialized logistics expertise, we streamline the supply chain process, including inventory management, warehousing, and order fulfilment. 

 Also, Critical follow-up on shipping documentation, LC requirements, customs clearance, transfer to W/Hs, communication with shipping lines and airlines, execution of shipments and trans-shipments on the hour, and can adapt to different delivery terms for both private and public shipments (KIMADIA-MOH- IRAQ 

Regulatory and Market Access

serves private & Tender and is headed by 1 Manager & Three Employees. Introducing new molecules to I.N.F., checking and submission of registration dossiers and manufacturing sites, & Performing Pharmacovigilance.

Finance and Accounting serves

 both private & Tender and is  headed  by  1 Manager. LAMASS PHARMA SDB uses a bank channel inside Iraq to finance its operations. Current payment terms are either L/C with deferred payment, a letter of guarantee, or advance payment  (sometimes called SBLC). 

Business Support

 consists mainly of the HR, IT, Administration & Engineering departments. Headed by 1 Manager & Four supervisors. Qualification of company's W/Hs to comply with G.S.P. regulations and anti-bribery and corruption training to comply with shared requirements from our principals.
